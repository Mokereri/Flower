About Us

Floral Wholesale Solutions

Founded in 2024, Edge Flowers is a leading global partner for wholesale Kenyan flowers.

Our vision is to be the leading global supplier of fresh, high-quality flowers, connecting markets worldwide with the beauty of nature. We strive to set new standards in the floral export industry through innovation, sustainability, and a commitment to excellence, creating lasting relationships and delivering unparalleled value to our customers.

Why Choose Edge Flowers?

Premium Quality Flowers

We ensure that every bloom is of the highest quality, handpicked and sourced from trusted growers globally. Our flowers are fresh, vibrant, and of superior quality, providing you with beautiful arrangements that last longer.

Global Reach

We offer international flower delivery, bringing your messages of love and care across borders. We have a reliable network to ensure your flowers arrive in perfect condition, locally or internationally.

Custom Floral Designs

We specialize in custom flower arrangements tailored to your occasion. Whether it’s for a wedding, birthday, or corporate gift, we craft designs that suit your style, preferences, and the message you wish to convey.

Sustainable Practices

We are committed to sustainable sourcing and environmentally-friendly practices. Our flowers are grown with minimal impact on the environment, ensuring every bouquet you send is eco-conscious.

Reliable Customer Service

Our customer service team is available 24/7 to assist you with any inquiries or issues you might have, ensuring you have a smooth and hassle-free experience.

Affordable Pricing with Exceptional Value

We offer competitive pricing for our premium flowers and services, ensuring that you get great value for your money with a transparent pricing model and no hidden fees.

Our Global Export Process

1

Order Placement & Confirmation

Secure your wholesale order and receive immediate confirmation of product availability and dispatch schedule.

2

Quality Assurance & Packing

Flowers are hand-selected, expertly graded, and carefully packed into temperature-controlled boxes.

3

Customs & Documentation

All necessary export documentation, phytosanitary certificates, and customs procedures are handled professionally.

4

Air Freight & Delivery

Fast and reliable air freight is used to ensure timely global delivery while maintaining the cold chain.

Step 5: Transportation & Shipping

Our flowers are carefully packed and shipped via our trusted global partners, ensuring the fastest and safest delivery options.

Step 6: Custom Documents

We handle all necessary documentation and customs clearance to ensure smooth, hassle-free international shipping.

Step 7: Follow Up

After delivery, we follow up to ensure customer satisfaction and to handle any queries you may have.
